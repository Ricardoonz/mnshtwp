FAQ

How did everything start?

We begin our journey from a connection between life plans and a dynamic of great
empathy. We started creating robots for innovation with artificial intelligence to get farther from
the offices and closer to people.

A few years of using technology, robotics and artificial intelligence to connect with the
people we love and a pandemic in the middle was enough to meet our initial call and
understand that we were ready for a bigger challenge.

Business Model

When someone buys our robot for personal use, it is permanently yours. We offer
additional cosmetic and functional upgrades for Anna, as an example. If the buyer is a Senior
Care Facility or a Hospital, there will be an option for a monthly subscription with a lower price.

Why are you interested in expanding in Canada?

We (Gabriel and Ricardo) are currently living in Richmond Hill and Ricardo is studying at
Centennial College, on the Software Engineering Technology – Artificial Intelligence program.
So, our objective is to bring to Canada the startups that are incorporated and already being
developed in Brazil, which we are remotely working for.

Why would I have a robot?

Because Anna will help you continue to live life with more longevity whether by
remembering your medicine schedules, full listening relationship and awareness or being
predictive when using artificial intelligence to process data from all your wearables on a single
platform.

What was the idea that originated Anna?

The Gabriel’s mother had a heart attack and almost passed away because:
1. There was no follow-up;
2. She fell and could not call anyone;
With this delay in asking for help, it aggravated her condition and made her more
weakened. That’s when the idea came up because, like his mother, there are thousands of
people who have experienced similar situations. So in addition to buying wearables for
monitoring, the idea was to create a robot that is an ally of these technologies, besides being
easy for elderly people to use;
